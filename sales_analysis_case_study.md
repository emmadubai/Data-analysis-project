Sales Performance Analysis Case Study


Objective


To identify sales trends, top-performing products, and revenue patterns using historical transaction data.


Business Questions




Which products generated the highest revenue?


What months produced the strongest sales performance?


Are there seasonal purchasing trends?




Approach




Collect sales records.


Clean and standardize data.


Analyze revenue trends.


Identify top-performing products.


Generate recommendations.




Findings




Premium products generated a disproportionate share of revenue.


Sales peaked during holiday periods.


Repeat customers contributed significantly to overall revenue.




Recommendations




Increase marketing during peak seasons.


Improve retention programs.


Expand inventory of high-performing products.
